Player Setup

  • Player 1 uses marker X.
  • Player 2 uses marker O.
  • Player 1 (X) always starts first.

Turns

  • Players take turns clicking an empty cell to place their marker.
  • A move is invalid if the cell is already occupied.

Winning Condition

  • A player wins by placing three of their markers in a row:
  • Horizontally (across any row)
  • Vertically (down any column)
  • Diagonally (corner to corner)

Draw Condition

  • If all 9 cells are filled and no player has won, the game ends in a draw.

Resetting

  • After a win or draw, the game board is disabled.
  • Use the Reset button to start a new match.
